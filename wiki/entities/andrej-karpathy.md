# Andrej Karpathy

AI researcher and educator. Former Director of AI at Tesla, founding member of OpenAI. Known for neural network lectures, minGPT, nanoGPT, and educational content on deep learning.

## Relevance to This Knowledge Base

Authored the [[concepts/llm-wiki-pattern]] — the foundational methodology this knowledge base follows. Published as a GitHub Gist on April 4, 2026, gathering 5,000+ stars in 3 days. (source: karpasky.md)

Key insight: he now spends more of his LLM token budget on manipulating knowledge stored as markdown files than on writing code. (source: karpasky.md)

His recommended workflow: LLM agent on one side, [[entities/obsidian]] on the other. "Obsidian is the IDE; the LLM is the programmer; the wiki is the codebase." (source: karpasky.md)
