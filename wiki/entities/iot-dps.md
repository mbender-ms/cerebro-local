# Overview of Azure IoT Hub Device Provisioning Service

The IoT Hub Device Provisioning Service (DPS) is a helper service for IoT Hub that enables zero-touch, just-in-time provisioning to the right IoT hub without requiring human intervention. In a cloud-based solution, DPS enables the provisioning of millions of devices in a secure and scalable manner.
